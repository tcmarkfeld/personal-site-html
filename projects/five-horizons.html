Five Spot by Five Horizons

Home > Portfolio > Five Horizons

Five Spot is a non-profit STD testing service in Alabama that provides outreach to their community. They needed a way to streamline their appointment process, and we provided that for them. Instead of having to call and book an appointment, people are now able to book on their app. It also shows appointment history, upcoming appointments, and educational facts provided by Five Spot.
